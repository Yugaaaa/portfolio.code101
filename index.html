How long does a project take?
Most projects are done within 7 to 14 days. Simpler sites can be faster — more complex ones may take a bit longer, but I'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do you help after launch?
Yes — every project comes with 30 days of post-launch support. If something breaks or needs tweaking, I'll sort it out at no extra cost.
Will it work on mobile?
Always. Every site I build is tested on phones, tablets, and desktops before delivery — mobile is not an afterthought, it's part of the design from day one.
